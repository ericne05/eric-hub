[TEST 4 - PART 2]
Type: Writing Task
Context: Yesterday you arranged to go to the cinema with Anne, an English friend. Unfortunately, you didn't get to the cinema on time and missed her. Write a note to Anne. In your note, you should: apologize for not meeting her, explain why you were late, suggest meeting another time.

Sample Answer:

Best,
[Your Name]
