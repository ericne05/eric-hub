[TEST 2 - PART 1]
Type: Sentence Building
Context: Rewrite the following sentences using the given words.

Question 1: Both/ father/ I/ love/ sailing.
Answer: Both my father and I love sailing.

Question 2: Grandparents/ give/ my father/ first boat/ but/ he/ not/ have/ it now.
Answer: My grandparents gave my father his first boat, but he does not have it now.

Question 3: Father/ want/ me/ join/ sailing course/ local sailing school.
Answer: My father wants me to join a sailing course at the local sailing school.

Question 4: I/ take/ first lesson/ last week.
Answer: I took my first lesson last week.

Question 5: It/ difficult/ but/ very/ interesting.
Answer: It was difficult but very interesting.

[TEST 2 - PART 2]
Type: Writing Task
Context: A friend, Eva, borrowed your rucksack two weeks ago and hasn't returned it. Write a note to Eva. In your note, you should: remind her that she borrowed your rucksack, ask her to return it, explain why you need it.

Sample Answer:
Hi Eva,
You borrowed my rucksack two weeks ago. Please can you return it to me soon? I need it because I am going on a trip this weekend. Let me know when you can bring it.
Thanks,
[Your Name]

[TEST 2 - PART 3]
Type: Writing Task
Context: You have just bought some new clothes. Write an email to your friend, Alex. In your email, you should: tell Alex what clothes you have bought, say where you bought the clothes from, explain why you needed to buy these clothes.

Sample Answer:
Hi Alex,
I have just bought a pair of blue jeans and a cool black jacket. I bought them at the market in the city center. I needed to buy them because I am going on holiday next week.
Best,
[Your Name]
