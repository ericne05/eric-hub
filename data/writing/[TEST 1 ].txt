[TEST 1 - PART 1]
Type: Sentence Building
Context: Rewrite the following sentences using the given words.

Question 1: There/ some/ advertisement/ jobs/ local newspaper.
Answer: There are some advertisements for jobs in the local newspaper.

Question 2: Fill/ job application form/ not/ take/ you/ long.
Answer: Filling in the job application form will not take you long.

Question 3: It/ important/ arrive/ work/ on time.
Answer: It is important to arrive at work on time.

Question 4: Some people/ like/ work/ outdoors/ than/ work/ inside.
Answer: Some people like working outdoors more than working inside.

Question 5: Some/ company/ give/ staff/ free/ uniforms.
Answer: Some companies give their staff free uniforms.

[TEST 1 - PART 2]
Type: Writing Task
Context: You are going to be absent from your English class next week. Write a note to your English teacher. In your note, you should: explain why you are going to be absent, apologize to your teacher, ask your teacher for information about next week's work.

Sample Answer:
Dear Teacher,
I am sorry but I will be absent from class next week because I have to go to the hospital. Please forgive me. Can you tell me what homework I need to do for the next lesson?
Thanks,
[Your Name]
