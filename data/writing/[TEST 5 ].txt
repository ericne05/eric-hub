[TEST 5 - PART 1]
Type: Sentence Building
Context: Rewrite the following sentences using the given words.

Question 1: The hairdresser/ last/ cut/ hair/ six/ month/ ago.
Answer: The hairdresser last cut my hair six months ago.

Question 2: When/ I/ phone/ she/ ask/ what time/ I/ want/ go.
Answer: When I phoned, she asked what time I wanted to go.

Question 3: My brother/ cut/ hair/ himself.
Answer: My brother cuts his hair himself.

Question 4: He/ say/ hairdresser/ too/ expensive/ him.
Answer: He says the hairdresser is too expensive for him.

Question 5: Unfortunately/ he/ not/ do/ it/ very/ good.
Answer: Unfortunately, he does not do it very well.

[TEST 5 - PART 2]
Type: Writing Task
Context: Your English friend has asked you to play tennis on Saturday, but you can't. Write an email to your friend. In your note, you should: apologize, say why you can't play tennis, suggest another time to play.

Sample Answer:

Best,
[Your Name]
