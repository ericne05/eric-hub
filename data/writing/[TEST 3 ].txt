[TEST 3 - PART 1]
Type: Sentence Building
Context: Rewrite the following sentences using the given words.

Question 1: Last week/ I/ go/ Ha Long city/ and/ stay/ Royal Lotus hotel.
Answer: Last week I went to Ha Long City and stayed at the Royal Lotus Hotel.

Question 2: This/ first time/ that/ I/ be/ here.
Answer: This is the first time that I have been here.

Question 3: The hotel/ big/ and/ has/ 10 floors.
Answer: The hotel is big and has 10 floors.

Question 4: It/ important/ book/ room/ in advance.
Answer: It is important to book a room in advance.

Question 5: My room/ big/ than/ my friend's room.
Answer: My room was bigger than my friend's room.
